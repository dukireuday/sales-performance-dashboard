Sales Performance Dashboard

Overview:

This project is about the analysis of business sales data to determine trends, best-performing products, and profitability trends.

Tools Used:
1)Power BI
2)Excel

Dashboard Features:

1)KPI Cards
2)Sales Trend Over Time
3)Sales by Category
4)Profit by Region
5)Top 10 Products by Sales
6)Sales vs Profit Analysis

Key Insights:

1)Technology is the best-performing category in terms of sales.
2)The best-performing region in terms of profit is the West region.
3)The sales are consistent over time.
4)There are some products with high sales but low profit.

Recommendations:

1)Focus on high-profit products.
2)Improve the pricing strategy for products with low profit.
3)Invest more in the best-performing region.

Files Included:

1)Sales_Dashboard.pbix
2)dataset.csv

Conclusion:

This project demonstrates how data analytics can be used to support business decision making through visualization and insights.
